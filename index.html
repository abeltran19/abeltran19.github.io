Our Intention

MediPod is designed to help healthcare workers keep up-to-date with the latest
technologies, discoveries, issues, and news in the medical world. MediPod allows
their users to either host their podcasts as they talk about any relevant topic
in the medical world, while also allowing others to listen and share their opinions
regarding the same topics.
